Privacy Policy

Hotter – Digital Business Card

Hotter respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection

Hotter does not automatically collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

If you choose to create a digital business card, you may voluntarily provide personal details (such as name, job title, or contact information). This information is shared only when you choose to share your digital card.

The app may collect limited, non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device and OS information
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash and performance data

Use of Information

Collected data is used only to:

  • Provide and improve app features
  • Ensure stability, security, and performance

Hotter does not use data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Anonymous analytics or crash data may be processed by trusted services (such as Firebase) for app improvement only.

Children’s Privacy

Hotter does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app means acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you do not agree with this policy, you may stop using the app at any time.
